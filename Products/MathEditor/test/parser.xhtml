MathML Parser

Type in text and math within two sets of $$ (that's 2 $ signs on each end) signs to auto-replace with MathML (similar to LaTeX). Example: 

The derivative of $$f_1$$ with respect to $$x$$ is $$x_1^2+2*c/2$$.

You can also have it pre-fill more complicated Math functions like integrate or sum by typing something like (note, there is no notation yet for the arguments of sum):

The sum of the numbers from $$1$$ to $$N$$ is $$sum=n*(n-1)/2$$
